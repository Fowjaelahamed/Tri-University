B.Sc in Computer Science & Engineering

Faculty

Engineering & Technology

Duration

4 Years

Program Description

Program: BSc (Honours) In Computer Science and Engineering

Admission: Students will be admitted to the department as per university rules.

Duration of the Program: 4 years.

Total Semesters: 4 X 2 = 8 (2 semesters a year of 22 weeks each).

Total Number of Credits in 8 semesters (4 years): 160.0.

Breakdown of each semester (of 22 weeks each):
Classes: 15 active weeks (1 day of each week must be reserved for makeup classes. If necessary, weekends can be used for makeup classes and extra/additional classes may be taken within the semester schedule to finish the course).
Break/PL: 2 weeks. No separate break for in-course examinations.
Semester Final Examinations: 2 weeks.
Evaluation of Scripts and Publication of Results: 3 weeks (2 weeks for grade submission and 1 week for tabulation and result publication).
Vacations: No separate semester break. Only the usual university vacations apply.

Teaching of the courses:
For each credit of a theory course, there will be 1 class per week of 1 hour duration.
Total classes in a semester for each credit of a theory course will be 15 (15 X 1).
Total Contact Hours in a semester for each 1.0 credit theory course: 15 X 1 = 15.
For each 1.0 credit lab course, there will be 1 class per week of 2 hours duration.
Total classes in a semester for each 1.0 credit lab course in 15 weeks: 15 X 1 = 15.
Total Contact Hours in a semester for each 1.0 credit lab course: 15 X 2 = 30.
